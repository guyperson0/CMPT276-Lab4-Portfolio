About Me

Hey there! As stated before, I'm Peter Liu, a 19 year old student in my second year of attending Simon Fraser University. I'm currently working on my Bachelor's in Science, with a major in Software Systems (which I like to joke is the same as Computer Science, but nobody knows what it is when you tell people your major). I enjoy writing, going on long walks, playing video games, and programming (most of the time). I believe my strength lies in my ability to persist through a tough challenge and to quickly learn things with hands on experience! Thanks for checking out my page!

Video Games!

I am, as most programmers I know are, an avid gamer. I primarily enjoy single-player games, with some favourites being Minecraft, Terraria, Enter the Gungeon, and Hades. I've taken two game-programming courses in high school, in which I made a static dungeon crawler in my first year and a 2D two-player PVP shooter in my second. I've also delved a bit into video game modding for Minecraft, although I have no published mods. To the right, you'll see the infamous guyperson1 block, named after my in-game username guyperson1, which explodes when mined! A lot of things in the mod explode. It's pretty awesome.
